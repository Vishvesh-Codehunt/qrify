FAQs

How to register?

On mobile, you can find out register link on navigation bar by tapping icon. Then fill out the information to become member of this site! Then you will be able to Log In with your email address and password.

How to scan QR code?

For Iphone users, you have in built QR scanner that you can use.But for android users, you have to download QR reader application from play store.

How I generate QR code for my own vehicle?

To generate QR code for your vehicle, You need to register on our site! after registering, you will see your dashboard that contain your QR code and details that you have filled.

Should I have to purchase QR code from your site?

Well, it is not necessary to purchase from our site.You can download or take a screen shot of your QR code and get print of that code and put in front of your car.But is you purchase from our site then many benefits you will get like reasonable price, proper format of the sticker.
